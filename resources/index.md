## Educational Resources

<div class="info-box note">
  <h4>Technical Frameworks</h4>
  <p>These frameworks provide simple ways to remember proper rowing technique:</p>
  
  <div class="two-col-grid">
    <div>
      <h5>KRAFT's Keys to Proper Technique</h5>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>K</strong>eep shoulders relaxed and down</li>
        <li><strong>R</strong>each from hips, not shoulders</li>
        <li><strong>A</strong>rms straight until legs are flat</li>
        <li><strong>F</strong>eather after extraction</li>
        <li><strong>T</strong>ap down before extraction</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    
    <div>
      <h5>Black Bear Sequence (BBS)</h5>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Grip and feather</strong> - Proper hand position</li>
        <li><strong>Release</strong> - Clean blade extraction</li>
        <li><strong>Recovery/Catch</strong> - Preparing for next stroke</li>
        <li><strong>Drive</strong> - Efficient power application</li>
        <li><strong>Steering</strong> - Maintaining course control</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
