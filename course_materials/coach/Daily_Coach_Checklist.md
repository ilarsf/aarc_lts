  <div id="preparation-tab" class="tab-content">
    <h2 id="pre-session">Pre-Session (30-45 minutes before class)</h2>

    <h3>Weather & Safety</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Check weather forecast and radar</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Assess water conditions (current, wind, visibility)</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Review safety boat status and equipment</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Verify first aid kit is stocked and accessible</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Check communication devices (phone, radio) are charged</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Equipment Preparation</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Identify and reserve appropriate boats for participants</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Inspect boats for any damage or issues</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Prepare necessary teaching aids and demo equipment</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Set up any land-based training equipment needed</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Organize PFDs and verify sufficient quantity</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Administrative</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Print roster and attendance sheet</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Review participant notes from previous session</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Prepare session plan with timing estimates</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Brief assistant coaches on daily objectives and assignments</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Prepare handout with links to AARC policy documents for participants' independent review:
        <ul>
          <li><input type="checkbox"> <a href="../../src/AARC_Safety_Rules.pdf" target="_blank">Safety Rules</a></li>
          <li><input type="checkbox"> <a href="../../src/AARC_Code_of_Conduct_2025-02-26.pdf" target="_blank">Code of Conduct</a></li>
          <li><input type="checkbox"> <a href="../../src/AARCSafeSport_2015-01-18.pdf" target="_blank">SafeSport Policy</a></li>
        </ul>
      </li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Prepare area for participant check-in</li>
    </ul>

    <h2 id="participant-arrival">Participant Arrival (15 minutes before class)</h2>

    <h3>Check-In</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Greet each participant by name</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Take attendance</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Collect any outstanding paperwork</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Direct participants to preparation area</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Identify any new injuries or concerns</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Orientation</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Conduct circle-up for announcements</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Review safety points relevant to day's conditions</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Outline session objectives and structure</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Demonstrate key technical focuses for the day</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Answer pre-session questions</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  
  <div id="execution-tab" class="tab-content">
    <h2 id="on-water-session">On-Water Session</h2>

    <h3>Launch Management</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Supervise equipment handling and boat carrying</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Oversee boat launching sequence</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Verify all boats have proper safety equipment</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Ensure safety boat is positioned appropriately</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Maintain visual contact with all participants</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Teaching Progression</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Introduce skills according to session plan</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Provide individual feedback to each participant</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Conduct group demonstrations as needed</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Monitor energy levels and adjust intensity</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Take mental notes on individual progress</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Safety Monitoring</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Track changing weather conditions</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Keep group appropriately consolidated</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Watch for signs of fatigue or discomfort</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Maintain awareness of other water traffic</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Be ready to assist with any capsizes or emergencies</li>
    </ul>

    <h2 id="return-to-dock">Return to Dock</h2>

    <h3>Landing Sequence</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Guide participants to return in appropriate order</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Supervise boat landing and removal from water</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Verify all participants and equipment return safely</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Oversee proper boat cleaning and storage</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Check for and document any equipment issues</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Immediate Debrief</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Gather group for brief technical feedback</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Highlight positive achievements</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Address common challenges observed</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Preview next session focus</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Answer any questions</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div id="session-tab" class="tab-content">
    <h2 id="session-specific-notes">Session-Specific Notes</h2>

    <h3>Session 1: Learning the Motion and Getting Wet</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Mention AARC policy documents for participants to review independently:
        <ul>
          <li><input type="checkbox"> <a href="../../src/AARC_Safety_Rules.pdf" target="_blank">Safety Rules</a></li>
          <li><input type="checkbox"> <a href="../../src/AARC_Code_of_Conduct_2025-02-26.pdf" target="_blank">Code of Conduct</a></li>
          <li><input type="checkbox"> <a href="../../src/AARCSafeSport_2015-01-18.pdf" target="_blank">SafeSport Policy</a></li>
        </ul>
      </li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Conduct erg session (grip, basic stroke, sequencing)</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Teach boat parts and equipment overview</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Practice safe boat handling (carrying, placing in slings)</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Demonstrate and conduct flip test for all participants</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Record flip test completion in participant files</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Practice dockside skills (getting in/out, stability)</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Teach ghost strokes while stabilized at dock</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Introduce equipment maintenance and storage</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Session 2: Basic Stroke Sequencing</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Review Day 1 learning points</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Conduct erg warm-up focusing on sequencing</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Teach equipment prep and oars-first approach</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Demonstrate and practice launching procedures</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Teach Pick Drill on water and recovery sequence</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Introduce pause drills for body awareness and control</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Practice basic turning using one-arm technique</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Teach proper landing procedure (approach, docking)</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Supervise post-row maintenance of equipment</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Session 3: Learning How to Push</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Review sequencing from previous session</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Conduct leg drive focus on ergs (Reverse Pick Drill)</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Teach river geography and traffic patterns</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Explain steering concepts and navigation awareness</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Contrast "good push" vs. "soft push" on water</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Practice applying leg drive on water</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Teach pivot turns for tighter maneuvering</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Check for appropriate looking frequency (3-5 strokes)</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Reinforce proper traffic pattern adherence</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Session 4: Navigation and Integration</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Review leg drive and navigation rules</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Explain bridge passage protocols and safety</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Assess rower control/confidence during warm-up</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Divide into appropriate skill groups if necessary</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Guide ready group through upriver navigation/bridges</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Continue skill development with developing group</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Conduct flip test re-takes for those who need it</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Observe ability to integrate steering with rowing</li>
      <li><input type="checkbox"> Discuss pathways after program completion</li>
    </ul>

    <h3 style="text-decoration: line-through;">Sessions 5-6: Not used in current program format</h3>
    <p><em>NOTE: The program now uses a 4-session format (3 hours each) rather than the previous 6-session format. Sessions 3 and 4 cover the integration and assessment components previously scheduled for Sessions 5-6.</em></p>
