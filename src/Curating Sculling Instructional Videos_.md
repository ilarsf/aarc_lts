# **Curated Video Syllabus: Learn to Scull**

This report presents a proposed video syllabus for the “Learn to Scull” course, designed to expand the existing video section into a structured and comprehensive library. For each topic, 1–3 high-quality, beginner-friendly instructional YouTube videos are recommended. These selections prioritize clarity, accuracy, credible instruction, and good production quality, with a preference for videos under 10 minutes where appropriate.

The following table outlines the syllabus, providing video titles, URLs, concise descriptions, and recommendations for incorporating them into the course. This resource aims to equip novice scullers with the foundational knowledge and skills for a safe and enjoyable experience on the water.
